`cautious_mos_bump` of **6%**, so today the required margin of safety is **18%**. In plain English:
the stock has to be on sale by nearly a fifth before we'll touch it.

  reports of an OpenAI IPO delay; semis off 2-4%. A falling tape can keep falling — no need to rush.
- **The long-term trend is still intact.** SPY $732.33 sits ~6% above its 200-day average. This is
  not a panic; it is simply not a bargain. The right move is to wait for better prices.
